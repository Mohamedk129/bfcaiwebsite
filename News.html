Benha University and Egyptian Space Agency Launches the Closing Ceremony for the Summer School for Space Science in its Second Edition

Benha University has organized the closing ceremony for the Summer School for Space Science in its Second Edition by Cooperation with Egyptian Space Agency. The Ceremony was held under auspices of Prof.Dr.Gamal Soussa, Benha University President and Dr.Mohamed El Qosy, the Executive manager for Egyptian Space Agency. The Ceremony was held in presence of Dr.Gamal El Saeed, The previous President of Benha University and Dr.Nasser El Gizawy, Benha University Vice President for Post Graduate Studies and Scientific Research and Prof. Tamer Samir, Benha University Vice-president for Education and Students 'Affairs. Prof.Dr.Gamal Soussa confirmed that the space science summer schools are one of the most important goals of the cooperation protocol that signed between Benha University and the Egyptian Space Agency, which aims to spread space science among Egyptian university students to graduate and prepare a qualified student for the labor market, raising the name of Egypt in all international forums. He also expressed his happiness with the success of the first and second Edition of the summer training schools which were organized by Benha University in cooperation with the Egyptian Space Agency.
